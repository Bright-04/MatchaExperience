About Matcha

The History of Matcha

Matcha is a finely ground powder of specially grown and processed green tea leaves. Unlike regular green tea, matcha contains the entire tea leaf, providing a more concentrated source of nutrients and antioxidants.

Originating from Japan, matcha has been used in traditional tea ceremonies for centuries and is now enjoyed worldwide for its unique flavor profile and health benefits.

Our Premium Quality

We source our matcha from the finest tea farms in Japan, ensuring exceptional quality, vibrant color, and authentic flavor with every cup.
